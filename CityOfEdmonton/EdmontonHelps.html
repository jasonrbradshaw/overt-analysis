Edmonton to the Rescue

On Tuesday, October 2, the City of Edmonton received a request for help from the City of Calgary to help Calgary deal with an unprecedented amount of snowfall (Calgary received almost 40cm in 24 hours!)

We sent 30 plows along with 60 staff down to Calgary to assist with snow clearing and road sanding. Thank you to our employees who stepped up last minute to help! Support is provided under our mutual aid agreements.

Special thanks to the City of Calgary and Kaitlin Lee 660NEWS for the images of our crews in Calgary.
